How It Works? (Customization)
Overview

The goal is to monitor and record real-time statistics on the color schemes that users prefer, providing valuable insights into user preferences and helping to optimize the visual experience based on these choices.

Playground

This section presents users with three theme options to choose from. Their selections are recorded in real time, allowing for the collection of comprehensive statistics on user color preferences.

Analytics Data

The data generated in the Playground area is instantly displayed here in a table format, complete with their corresponding statuses.

Chart View

This section displays the graph data as percentage averages, presented in a visually intuitive format to enhance clarity and support effortless interpretation.
